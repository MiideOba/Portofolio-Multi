Part 2

Leadership Journey - During years on campus, i was actively involved in student leadership and made a significant mark in service. From the earliest days, i served as Class Representative from 100 to 500 level, later becoming a two-term OOUSU Senator, Coordinator of the Departmental Association, Vice President of NAAS OOU, and eventually the 35th President of the Association. I also served as Vice President of the Leo Club, Ayetoro Campus and was an active member of the Mental Health Support Initiative (MHSI) and the Muslim Students’ Society of Nigeria (MSSN). My leadership journey was marked by impactful programs and events, including the NAAS Agro-Summit 2024 featuring the reknowned Farmer Akin Alabi, the NAAS Ojude Faaji Cultural Festival, first ever Freshers’ Week in OOU, the NAAS Super League and 5-aside football competitions, the Provost Cup Tournament and awareness campaigns such as the War Against Drug Abuse Seminar in partnership with NDLEA. I also initiated welfare projects, tutorial classes, student security advocacy with the Police and DSS, and mobilized fundraising efforts for indigent students. My contributions and dedication to service earned me multiple awards and recognitions. I was honored as the Executive of the Year by NAAS OOU, received the Award of Excellence in Leadership at the OOU Leadership Conference, was named Most Influential Senator by NAAS OOU, and gained an award of recognition for my contributions to student welfare.

I also shared in the recognition of the Clique of the Year (Bat Boys) and though nominated twice as Best Class Rep in the OOU SUG Awards, i narrowly missed the win, yet the nominations reflected my impact. I also holds certifications as a First-Tier Responder in Mental Health (MHSI) and Soft Skills Training (Jobberman Nigeria).

My ability to balance leadership responsibilities with academic excellence speaks to my resilience and dedication. Beyond graduation, i continues to grow as an agri-tech enthusiast, operation person, volunteer, writer and public speaker. My professional interests now cover operations management, sustainable agriculture, administration, logistics and procurement, sustainability, project coordination, digital communication, and human resources. I have a strong foundation in agriculture and research, I am still passionate about agricultural extension, ICT in Agriculture, sustainable agriculture and delivering impactful solutions in agriculture and across diverse industries.
